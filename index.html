✦ Luck Token Tracker ✦
SoloDark Rule: Gain a luck token on a natural 20. You can have as many luck tokens as you have PCs in your group. New PCs begin with one luck token. Spend to auto-crit, reroll, or move up in initiative.
Luck Uses (Shadowdark)
All Classes: Auto-crit · Reroll any roll · Move up in initiative
Fighter: Extra attack · Take ½ damage targeting ally
Ranger: Prepare any remedy · Set alarm (near area, 8 hrs)
Thief: Apply backstab · Throw caltrops
Wizard: Obscuring circle (8 hrs) · Cast failed spell (take d4 dmg)
Priest: Create d4 holy water · Consecrate near area
Barbarian: Extra attack · Fight until death timer = 0
